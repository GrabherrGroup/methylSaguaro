Welcome to methylSaguaro!

methylSaguaro is an unsupervised machine learning method that segments the genome into distinct methylation patterns.
methylSaguaro is based on Saguaro, which was designed to discover selective forces in the whole genome population analyses, combining Hidden Markov Models and Neural Networks. It works with WGBS data or any type of methylation arrays.
